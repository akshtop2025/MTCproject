Course Highlights:

  • Live demonstrations of ultrasound assessment in Pregnancy , Obstetrics, Gynec Condition, and Infertility

  •  Lectures and video presentations on ultrasound in Pregnancy  Obstetrics and Gynaecology and Infertility

What will you learn:

FOGSI: (Basic or Advance Cases)

  • Opportunity to learn, observe & discuss the process and protocols with the expert trainers and clear your doubts with them. 

  •  Physics of Ultrasonography & Machine Settings

  • Basics of Early Pregnancy ultrasound & Dating at 8-10 weeks scan

  • Pregnancy of uncertain viability & unknown location

  • Fetal Biometry & Growth Charts

  • Cervical Assessment for Preterm Labour

  • Basics of Gynec Ultrasound: Uterus, Ovaries, Adnexa

  • How to do reporting after a scan

  • Advance: Rs. 29,500/- (including GST)  - 14 Days
