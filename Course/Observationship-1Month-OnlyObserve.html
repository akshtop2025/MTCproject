About the course

Welcome to the Clinical Art and Embryology Course offered by Motherhood Training Center. Our premier program is dedicated to advancing your knowledge in reproductive medicine. The 1-month Observationship provides a unique opportunity to learn from top experts in the field through exclusive observation. Join us to gain valuable insights and enhance your understanding of clinical art and embryology practices.

Course Highlights:

Andrology Lab Techniques and Management

  • Semen Analysis Methods

  • Sperm Preparation Techniques for ART

  • Semen Analysis Methods

  • Cryopreservation of sperm samples

  • Quality control in Andrology Labs

  • Lab management and record-keeping

Embryo Culture Systems

  • Types of culture media

  • Optimal Environmental Conditions for Embryo Growth

  • Monitoring Embryo development

  • Traditional vs. advanced culture systems

  • Troubleshooting common culture issues

Oocyte Scanning and Handling

  • Oocyte Identification and Assessment

  • Proper handling techniques

  • Evaluation of Oocyte quality

  • Preparation of Oocytes for fertilisation

Vitrification

  • Principles of Vitrification

  • Step-by-step Vitrification Protocols

  • Use of various Vitrification kits

  • Factors influencing Vitrification success

  • Warming procedures and post-thaw assessment

ICSI (Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection)

  • Sperm selection for ICSI

  • Micromanipulation techniques

  • ICSI procedure step-by-step

  • Post-ICSI care of injected oocytes

  • Assessing fertilization after ICSI

Embryo Transfer

  • Embryo selection criteria

  • Fresh vs. Frozen Embryo Transfer Protocols

  • Transfer techniques and tools

  • Factors influencing successful implantation

  • Post-transfer patient care

Embryology Lab Management (QA/QC):

  • Setting up QA/QC protocols

  • Maintaining clean room standards

  • Equipment maintenance and calibration

  • Risk management in the Embryology lab

  • Regulatory compliance

  • Data management and reporting

  • Strategies for continuous improvement

What will you learn:

Observationship - 1 Month - Only Observe

  •  Andrology lab management (QA/QC)

  •  Embryology lab management (QA/QC)

  •  Semen analysis Sperm wash technique

  • Sperm freezing

  •  OPU (Oocyte screening)

  •  ET (Embryo transfer)

  • Vitrification -Thawing

  • ICSI

  •  Embryo culture
