About the course

This intensive laparoscopy course, offered by the Motherhood Training Center (MTC), provides a comprehensive and hands-on approach to mastering Gynecological laparoscopic surgery. Designed for Gynecologists and surgeons seeking to enhance their minimally invasive surgical skills, this program provides a unique blend of theoretical knowledge and practical experience.

Motherhood Training Center's course begins with foundational principles and progresses to advanced techniques, covering the full spectrum of Gynecological Laparoscopic procedures. Participants will have the opportunity to learn, observe, and perform both basic and advanced gynecological surgeries in Motherhood Women’s and Child Care Hospital’s state-of-the-art operation theater environment.

A key feature of Motherhood Training Center's program is its emphasis on hands-on experience. Fellow Doctors will actively participate in live surgeries, gradually transitioning from observation to assisted performance, and ultimately to independently conducting procedures under expert supervision. This practical experience is complemented by intensive training sessions using Endo Trainers, allowing participants to refine their skills in a controlled, simulated environment.

The course incorporates both 2D and 3D Laparoscopic Systems, ensuring that participants are well-versed in the latest technological advancements in the field. This dual-system approach provides a comprehensive understanding of depth perception and spatial orientation crucial for successful laparoscopic surgery.

Didactic lectures delivered by MTC's experienced surgeons will cover topics ranging from patient selection and preoperative planning to advanced surgical techniques and postoperative care. These lectures are reinforced by live surgery demonstrations, offering real-time insights into surgical decision-making and technique application.

A unique aspect of Motherhood Training Center's course is its focus on the entire perioperative process. Participants will learn the intricacies of pre-and post-operative management, including patient workup, understanding surgical protocols, and managing potential complications. This holistic approach ensures that fellow doctors are prepared not just for the technical aspects of surgery, but for comprehensive patient care.

The program concludes with a thorough evaluation process, utilizing Endotrainers to assess the skills acquired throughout the course. This pre- and post-evaluation system allows participants to measure their progress and identify areas for further improvement.

By the end of our tailor-made Laparoscopy course, participants will have gained the knowledge, skills, and confidence necessary to incorporate Advanced Laparoscopic Techniques into their surgical practice, ultimately improving patient outcomes and expanding their professional capabilities in minimally invasive Gynecological Surgery.

Course Highlights:

Surgical Observation and Performance:

  • Learning, observing, and performing basic Gynecological Laparoscopic surgeries

  • Progressing to advanced Gynecological Laparoscopic procedures

  • Hands-on experience in the operating theater

Didactic Education:

  • Lectures covering Laparoscopic theory, techniques, and best practices

  • Live surgery demonstrations

Simulation Training:

  • Endotrainer sessions under expert supervision

  • Pre-evaluation training using Endotrainers to assess initial skill level

  • Post-evaluation training to measure improvement and ensure competency

Technology Familiarization:

  • Training in 4k 3D RUBINA Laparoscopic systems

  • Advanced training in 3D Laparoscopic systems

Perioperative Care:

  • Pre-operative workup for Laparoscopic Surgeries

  • Post-operative management of patients

  • Understanding and implementing Laparoscopic Surgery protocols

Hands-on Surgical Experience:

  • Practical, supervised experience during actual Laparoscopic surgeries

  • Progression from assisting to performing procedures

What will you learn:

FELLOWSHIP (Hands-on): Basic Laparoscopy

  • Diagnostic Laparoscopy

  • Laparoscopic Tubal Occlusion

  • Salpingectomy for Tubal Pregnancy

  • Simple Adhesiolysis

  • Salpingectomy for Hydrosalpinx with Mild Pelvic Adhesions

  • Ovarian Drilling

  • Aspiration / Fenestration of Cyst

FELLOWSHIP(Hands-on): Advanced Laparoscopy

  • Diagnostic Laparoscopy

  • Laparoscopic Tubal Occlusion

  • Salpingectomy for Tubal Pregnancy

  • Simple Adhesiolysis

  • Ovarian Drilling

  • Aspiration / Fenestration of Cyst

  • Total laparoscopic hysterectomy

  • Use of tissue morcellator

  • Suture of vaginal vault

  • Specimen retrieval techniques

  • Salpingectomy for Hydrosalpinx with Mild Pelvic Adhesions

  • Hassan technique, Palmer's point entry in previously operated difficult case entries
