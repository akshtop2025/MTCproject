About Us

Motherhood Training Center - A subsidiary branch -of Motherhood Women’s & Child Care Hospital, Ahmedabad offers FOGSI-certified as well as fellowship (Hands-On) courses to practising Obstetricians and Gynaecologists, freshly graduated Gynecologists, and those who want to elevate their practice for industry expertise in the fields of Infertility, Sonography, Laparoscopy, and Embryology.

MTC is the brainchild of the director of Motherhood Women’s & Child Care Hospital - Dr. Anand Patel. After decades of industry experience, he found a substantial disparity between the growing needs of patients and the availability of proficient Gynaecologists and Obstetricians capable of meeting these demands.

Dr. Twinkal Patel took it upon herself to manifest this idea into fruition and handled the responsibility of designing and executing Motherhood Training Center courses along with the support and technical guidance of all the MTC expert trainers.



The training center aims to equip medical professionals with the skills and knowledge required to meet the intricate challenges posed by Gynaecology and Obstetrics. The focus of the center revolves around providing comprehensive training through flexible and customized courses.
