Dr. Shital Punjabi

Senior IVF specialist, Senior Gynecologist

Profile :

Dr. Shital Punjabi is a distinguished IVF Specialist with a Gold Medal MD, DGO, and an MBA. Her expertise spans over 27 years in Gynecology, with advanced training in IVF and Embryology from Cleveland Clinic, Ohio. Furthermore, She is also a FOGSI-CERTIFIED SONOLOGIST.

Dr Punjabi is also a Cosmetic Gynecology expert at Midas Touch Cosmetic Centre.
