Dr. Rajesh Punjabi

Senior Consultant Gynaecologist & Obstetrician, Laparoscopic Surgeon & IVF Specialist

Profile :

With over 25 years of distinguished experience in Gynecology and Surgery, Dr. Rajesh Punjabi is a respected figure in his field.

At Motherhood Training Center, Dr. Punjabi brings this wealth of experience and holistic approach to healthcare, enriching our educational programs and inspiring the next generation of medical professionals.

Education and Training :

  • Practising Gynecologist & Laparoscopic surgeon. MD, CICE (France).

Experience :

  • Director, IVF Programme, Motherhood Women's Hospital, Ahmedabad (2002 - Present).

  • Director, Shashwat IVF Center Pvt. Ltd., Ahmedabad (2009 - Present).

  • Senior Gynecologist, Shashwat Hospital, Ahmedabad (1996 - Present).

  • Director, Midas Touch Cosmetic & Aesthetic Centre, Ahmedabad (2015 - Present).

Professional Achievements

  • Preventive Healthcare: Dr. Punjabi is dedicated to making preventive care accessible to underserved populations, recognizing its crucial role in overall public health.

  • Geriatric Mental Wellbeing: He is actively involved in initiatives promoting mental health and support for elderly and socially isolated individuals.

Motherhood Training Center - A subsidiary of Motherhood Women’s & Child Care Hospital, offers FOGSI-certified as well as fellowship (Hands-On) courses to practising Obstetricians and Gynecologists for industry expertise in the fields of Infertility, Sonography, Laparoscopy and Embryology.
